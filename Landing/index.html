About

Itachi Uchiha is a character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto.

He is the older brother of Sasuke Uchiha, and is responsible for killing all the members of their clan, sparing only Sasuke.

Itachi was a member of the Uchiha Clan of Konohagakure and eventually became a Konoha missing-nin.

He was a member of the Akatsuki2.He was considered a prodigy by the Uchiha due to his talents and intelligence
